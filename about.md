# Draggable Popups
by [undefined0](user:13351341)

---

This mod simply lets you drag popups around! Useful in the editor to preview effects or objects without having to close
the popup you're editing. Use the scroll wheel (or pinch) to change the scale of popups, and drag the popup to the
bottom middle to dismiss it (configurable in the mod settings).

Keybinds are configurable on desktop (Right Click by default), but are fixed to a two-finger drag on mobile.
